Chicken Stroganoff

A delicious meal made with chicken breast and lots of milk cream, done grandma's way.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 1 can of milk cream
  • 1 can of tomato extract
  • 1 bottle of ketchup
  • 1 chicken broth cube
  • Salt and spice to taste

Directions

  1. Cut your chicken breast into cubes
  2. Season your chicken with the spices you want
  3. Put the oil into the oven and let it heat
  4. When it's heated enough you can throw your chicken pieces and add just a little bit of water
  5. When the chicken is "white coloured" you can put the chicken broth cube and let it melt
  6. Put on the tomato extract and a little bit of your ketchup
  7. Put on the can of milk cream when you serve

You did it! Now you can enjoy one of the best foods you'll ever have in your life.
